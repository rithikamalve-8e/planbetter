# Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Start Using Right Now (30 seconds)

Your Green Floor Plan Optimizer is **ready to use immediately** - no configuration needed!

### Step 1: Open the App
The app is already running and ready.

### Step 2: Upload a Floor Plan
1. Click **"Get Started"** on the landing page
2. Drag & drop a floor plan image (or click "Browse Files")
3. Supported formats: PNG, JPG (up to 10MB)
4. ✅ Upload completes instantly!

### Step 3: Add Your Preferences (Optional)
1. Describe what matters to you in the text box
2. Example: *"Focus on energy efficiency and natural lighting"*
3. Or leave it blank for default recommendations

### Step 4: Analyze
1. Click **"Analyze Floor Plan"**
2. Wait 2-3 seconds
3. ✅ Get 6 detailed sustainability recommendations!

### Step 5: Explore Recommendations
- View energy savings and CO₂ reduction for each suggestion
- Toggle recommendations on/off to see their impact
- See real-time metrics updates
- View before/after impact charts

### Step 6: Download Report
1. Click **"View Full Report"**
2. Review comprehensive analysis
3. Click **"Download Report"** for a text file
4. ✅ Share with contractors or architects!

## 🎯 Tips for Best Experience

### For Floor Plan Images:
- Use clear, high-resolution images
- Architectural drawings work best
- Photos of sketches also work fine
- Make sure walls and windows are visible

### For Preferences:
- Be specific about your priorities
- Mention budget if you have one
- Include climate/location details
- Specify renovation vs. new build

### Example Preference Input:
```
I'm renovating a 1960s home in a cold climate.
Priorities:
- Reduce heating costs by 30%
- Maximize natural light
- Budget: $20,000
- Use sustainable materials
```
